Aalto University's Bachlor(+Master) of Science

Aalto Bachelor's Programme in Science and Technology

It has five study options.

  1. Chemical Engineering
  2. Computational Engineering
  3. Data Science
  4. Digital Systems and Design
  5. Quantum Technology

The ones might fit me the best are Computational Engineering and Data Science.

Computaional Engineering study has three focuses:

  1. Mathematics
  • Integral and differential calculus
  • Linear algebra
  • Statistical analysis
  1. Engineering principles.
  • Fluid mechanics
  • Thermodynamics
  • Mechanics of materials
  1. Computing.
  • Programming principles and algorithms
  • Numerical methods
  • Finite Element method

Admission Process​

Reference: 2023 Spring Timeline​

25 January 2023 at 3pm/15.00 (GMT +2:00) -- Need to have RP by then.

Deadline for submitting documents proving the applicant’s liability to pay tuition fees or exemption from tuition fees

AND

Deadline for submitting documents proving eligibility (applicants who have graduated before spring 2023 and applicants completing a foreign qualification granting eligibility to higher education studies in spring 2023) --URV diploma certificate
