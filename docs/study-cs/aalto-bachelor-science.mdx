[Aalto Bachelor's Programme in Science and Technology](https://www.aalto.fi/en/study-at-aalto/admission-to-aalto-bachelors-programme-in-science-and-technology-bachelor-and-master-of-science-in)

It has five study options.
1. Chemical Engineering
2. Computational Engineering
3. Data Science
4. Digital Systems and Design
5. Quantum Technology

The ones might fit me the best are [**Computational Engineering**](https://www.aalto.fi/en/study-options/computational-engineering-bachelor-of-science-technology-master-of-science-technology) and **Data Science**. 

Computaional Engineering study has three focuses:
1. Mathematics 
- Integral and differential calculus
- Linear algebra
- Statistical analysis

2. Engineering principles. 

- Fluid mechanics
- Thermodynamics
- Mechanics of materials

3. Computing. 

- Programming principles and algorithms
- Numerical methods
- Finite Element method


The course curriculum can be found [here](https://www.aalto.fi/en/programmes/aalto-bachelors-programme-in-science-and-technology/curriculum-2022-2024)
